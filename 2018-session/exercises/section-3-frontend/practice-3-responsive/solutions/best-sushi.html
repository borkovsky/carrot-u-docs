The Best Japanese Food in SF

Best Sushi

  • Akiko’s Restaurant & Sushi Bar

    Definitely want to get the Omakase. The Chefs prepare many dishes that are not on the menu, which make it really special. Don't worry, it will be a lot of food!

    Akiko’s Website

  • Pabu

    The sushi is amazing, but don't be afraid to order the other dishes as there are many of classic Izakaya dishes being served. Pro tip: they have a great weekday happy hour with many of their best izakaya dishes and some of their sake for a great price!

    Pabu's Website

  • Zushi Puzzle

    Tiny hidden gem that is a must! Just be prepared to wait — Chong and his wife are the only ones preparing food for the entire restaurant.

    Zushi Puzzle's Website
